About Me

I have grown up in Kolkata and have completed my Masters' and Bachelors' in Computer Applications from here. I have started my career in February 2013 and currently working as a Software Development Manager in Seamless Distribution Systems AB.

In this tenure of my life, I have worked on multiple languages, Tools and Frameworks like C#, Java, Shell Scripts, Dockers, Spring etc. and also having my hands on differnt domains like Networking, Telecom, Sales and Distributions.

I always try to learn new things because I feel, in this era of microservice architectures if I understand the "What" and "Why" part of a particular technology or concept then the "how" part can be done easily by using any of the languages available in the market and best fitted for business goals.
